SCHOOL TRIP

The NVM wants everybody to be able to make Videogames!
Through our learning programmes we aim to encourage and enable engagement with
videogames in lots of different environments. Schools, clubs, family-learning,
corporate training - there’s a place for everyone in our learning.
We are proud to have welcomed thousands of students from all around the country
giving them the chance to play loads of
interactive exhibitions that celebrate and challenge videogame culture.
We Want To Inspire New People, To Make New Games About New Kinds Of Subjects!

PIXELHEAD

Pixelheads is a community for young people who want to play games,
talk & learn about games and create their own. The scheme gives
young people aged 8 – 14 access to an exciting and exclusive programme
of events including workshops, talks and socials building
a community of like-minded peers.
